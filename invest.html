Golden Rules of Investing

Follow these principles to invest smarter.

  • 1

    Never invest money you can't afford to lose

    Always have an emergency fund (3-6 months expenses) before investing. Your rent money is not investment money.

  • 2

    Diversify your portfolio

    Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Spread investments across different asset classes to reduce risk.

  • 3

    Think long-term

    Time in the market beats timing the market. Consistent investing over years builds real wealth.

  • 4

    Do your own research

    Understand what you're investing in. Don't follow hype or social media tips blindly.

  • 5

    Start small, learn, then scale

    Begin with small amounts. Learn from your wins and losses before committing larger sums.

  • 6

    Keep emotions out of investing

    Fear and greed are your worst enemies. Stick to your strategy and avoid panic selling or FOMO buying.
