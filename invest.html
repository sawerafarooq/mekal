The classic! Drop your coins in and watch it fill up. Zero risk, but also zero growth.

👑 Money Rules Every Kid Should Know

Follow these simple rules and you'll be a money genius!

  • 🛑

    Only use money you don't need right now

    If you need that money for something important, don't invest it. Only use extra money.

  • 🥚

    Don't put all your eggs in one basket

    Spread your money around! If one goes down, you still have the others.

  • 🐢

    Slow and steady wins the race

    Don't try to get rich quick. Let your money grow slowly over many years!

  • 📚

    Learn before you spend

    If someone says "trust me, it's easy money" - be careful! Do your homework first.

  • 🌱

    Start small like a seed

    Even $5 can be invested! Start small, learn, and grow your money garden.

  • 🧘

    Stay calm - don't panic!

    Sometimes money goes down a little. That's normal! Be patient, it usually comes back.
